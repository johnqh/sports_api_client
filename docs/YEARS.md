# Available Seasons by Sport (Free Plan)

This document lists the available seasons for each sport API based on testing with a free plan API key.

**Note:** Free plans have limited access to historical data. Paid plans may have access to additional seasons.

## API-Football (Soccer)

**Available seasons:** 2008 - 2027

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years (e.g., `2024`, `2025`).

## API-Basketball

**Available seasons:** 2008 - 2027

Seasons can be in two formats:
- Single year: `2024`
- Season range: `2024-2025`

The format depends on the league (NBA uses ranges, international leagues may use single years).

## API-Hockey

**Available seasons:** 2008 - 2026

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-NFL (American Football)

**Available seasons:** 2010 - 2025

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-Baseball

**Available seasons:** 2008 - 2026

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-Rugby

**Available seasons:** 2008 - 2027

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-Formula-1

**Available seasons:** 2012 - 2025

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-Handball

**Available seasons:** 1970, 2008 - 2026

Note: There is historical data from 1970, then a gap until 2008.

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-Volleyball

**Available seasons:** 2008 - 2026

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

## API-MMA

**Available seasons:** 2022 - 2025

Seasons are represented as 4-digit years.

**Note:** MMA API has limited historical data compared to other sports.
